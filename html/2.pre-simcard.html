How to Activate Your SIM Card
  1. Insert the SIM card into your phone
  2. Turn on your phone and wait for network registration
  3. Follow the activation instructions provided
  4. Test your connection by making a call or using data
  5. Save important contacts and emergency numbers

Tips for Travelers

Before Your Trip

  • Check if your phone is unlocked
  • Research SIM card options in advance
  • Backup your current SIM card
  • Download offline maps and translation apps

In China

  • Keep your passport with you when buying SIM
  • Ask for English instructions if needed
  • Test your connection immediately
  • Keep your SIM card receipt safe
